MAP HOTEL AMAROOSSA ROYAL BOGOR

MAP

The Amaroossa Hotel Royal Bogor is strategically located in the heart of the city in Otto Iskandardinata street, providing excellent and convenient access to all of the attractions throughout the city. Located in a beautiful area in the downtown city center, the hotel enjoys a commanding location in an area full of restaurants and shopping opportunities as well as numerous facilities for family fun. The hotel is within easy reach of numerous attractions offered by this lively and friendly city, including Giant Botani Square, Suryakencana (Chinatown), the Bogor Botanical Gardens, Bogor Palace and the SKI Tajur waterpark, among others.
